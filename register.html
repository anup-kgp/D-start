Kharagpur Mini Marathon 2026.

Organized by Kharagpur D Star Athletic Club (KDSAC), a Fit India Registered Youth Club. This standalone page is for the official in-person paid mini marathon at Kharagpur Sersa Stadium, Paschim Medinipur, West Bengal, India.

Event day: 10 May 2026 (Sunday)
Registration window: 5 April to 30 April 2026
Reporting 5:00 AM • Race start 6:00 AM
Women 5 KM (14+) • Men 10 KM (15+)
Venue and format

Venue: Kharagpur Sersa Stadium, Paschim Medinipur, West Bengal, India. This is an in-person paid event with official prizes and finisher benefits for all participants.

Basic medical kit support will be available. Participants take part at their own risk; organizers are not responsible for any injury or accident.

Available Categories

Only two race categories are available for this marathon.

Prizes & participant benefits

Men 10 KM prizes: ₹10,000 / ₹7,000 / ₹5,000 / ₹2,000 / ₹1,000. Women 5 KM prizes: ₹5,000 / ₹4,000 / ₹3,000 / ₹2,000 / ₹1,000. All participants receive a T-shirt, finisher medal, e-certificate, breakfast, bib number, and hydration support.
